Dubai - United Arab Emirates

August 2019, A Recap on The City of Gold

By Ashleigh

Dubai is a city located in the United Arabic Emirates and is the most populated in the UAE. This city is well known for its luxurious shopping, nightlife and state-of-the-art architecture. The currency used is Arab Emirate Dirham (AED) and religion of the land is Islam.

Fun Facts About Dubai

Interesting Things to Know!

  • Did you know that Dubai is the home to over 200 nationalities.

  • The Burj Khalifa is the worlds tallest building.

  • 50 years ago Dubai was a small town in the desert.

Places You Must Visit

My Top 3 Picks

  1. The Burj Khalifa: Burj Khalifa is the world's largest freestanding structure, measuring up to 2,716ft tall. It contains restaurants, a hotel and observation decks. During the evening there are building and fountain displays for entertainment.

  2. Desert Safari: Desert Safari tours are full of so many activities such as quad biking, belly dancing, camel riding, sandboarding etc. There are a range of packages to choose from and all include hotel pick up so you don't have to worry about transport. There is also the option to camp or enjoy a barbeque cuisine whilst you are there.

  3. Aquaventure Waterpark: This waterpark is the biggest and most popular waterpark in Dubai. It is located in the Atlantis hotel and measure up to 17 hectares. Aquaventure also holds a 700m stretch of a luxurious private sand beach.

My Personal Highlight

The Dubai Mall

If you are a big shopper and love good food, you must visit the The Dubai Mall. There are a wide range of shops and restaurants available.
